🧐 What is Vote Cast?

Vote Cast is a lightweight web application designed to run single-stage online voting sessions. It helps organizations and
communities collect decisions, preferences, or opinions in a simple and democratic way. Use it in:

  • Local initiatives & public communities
  • Political groups & civic organizations
  • Companies, teams, unions, and beyond

⚙️ How Voting Works

  • Admin creates a session and sets available voting options
  • All signed-up users are notified via email
  • Voting is anonymous to ensure privacy
  • Only authenticated users can vote

🔐 Why Use Vote Cast?

  • Transparent and fair voting mechanics
  • Designed for simplicity — no distractions
  • Focused on privacy, security, and accessibility
Vote Cast is ideal for small to mid-sized groups who need a quick and trustworthy way to gather votes online.
